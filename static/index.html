What are the Kolam Rules?

  • Begin with a grid of dots — symmetry is essential.
  • Draw continuous lines looping around the dots.
  • Kolam “seeds” and depth parameters produce endless patterns.
  • Every Kolam blends art, tradition, and mathematics!

How to Use KolamVerse

KolamTrace: Upload a Kolam image to trace, convert, and watch an animated step-by-step rendering.

KolamDraw: Generate new Kolam patterns using seed codes and pattern depth, experiment and discover new designs!
